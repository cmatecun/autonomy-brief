Once you have Volume 3, the cheapest next step is to mail it to https://overnight-scanning.eu/ [ https://substack.com/redirect/d26b5662-16d7-4b43-9339-872218017a40?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] to digitize into PDF. Once you have the PDF, email it to scott@slatestarcodex.com.
I’ll give a free lifetime subscription to whoever accomplishes this - but if you’re interested, email me first so I can confirm that nobody else has beaten you to it.
