And whether you live there or not, consider donating [ https://substack.com/redirect/bfd25a07-83c5-4c03-bc31-c23bb246826f?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] or filling out this form [ https://substack.com/redirect/29a35b67-a3ff-4259-a80b-3a6d83827617?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] to potentially volunteer.
