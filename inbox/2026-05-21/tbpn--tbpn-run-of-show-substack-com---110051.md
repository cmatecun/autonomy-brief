An Absolute Flood of News from Tech Companies Today
SpaceX filed its S-1 [ https://substack.com/redirect/77bd4384-bd04-4656-b7ea-93af55d7b447?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], a comprehensive prospectus on the business before going public. Anthropic is set to have its first profitable quarter [ https://substack.com/redirect/1bee3445-6b29-4eb8-86bc-eb4989f84b39?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] as it expects revenue to surge 130% to $10.9B in Q2 2026. The company disclosed these figures to investors in their ongoing fundraise.  
WSJ reports [ https://substack.com/redirect/9199d052-9e63-4c48-90a7-6a47441054db?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] that OpenAI is working with Goldman and Morgan Stanley to file for an IPO as soon as this week, and that Sam ultimately wants to be able to go public by September of this year. Also, the company announced yesterday that it had achieved a breakthrough [ https://substack.com/redirect/2afbcf45-e242-4767-8cf4-0d9f631b2b30?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] on a decades-old math problem, the planar unit distance problem, using a general-purpose internal model. 
Fueled by parabolic compute demand, Nvidia posted another record quarter yesterday [ https://substack.com/redirect/c6bd3db8-d6bb-4799-9448-dcbac958d3e8?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], reporting that it had 85% YoY sales growth, beating analysts’ 79% expectation. The company also announced an $80B stock buyback authorization. 
Yesterday Meta began the process of cutting 8,000 jobs [ https://substack.com/redirect/5d1e4015-ae1d-4aa5-99a5-6e35d0ce71c1?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], or 10% of its workforce, as a means of offsetting its costs associated with AI. Zuck says he doesn’t expect any more RIFs this year.
