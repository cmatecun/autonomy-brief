🟣 We’re Live With The Collison Brothers 
Today’s the second day of Stripe Sessions 2026, the payment company’s annual conference. Yesterday, they unveiled nearly 300 new products and features [ https://substack.com/redirect/f51d9f24-780f-4aea-80ec-14e0a5b445d6?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] to an audience of over 9,000 entrepreneurs and builders. The focus of this year’s Sessions has so far been very much AI and agentic AI. Among other new features, yesterday the company announced:
Agentic Commerce Suite, which lets businesses sell through AI agents by uploading product catalogs and managing agent access from the Stripe Dashboard
Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP), a partnership with Google that will soon allow customers to buy products in “AI Mode” and the Gemini app
Machine Payments Protocol (MPP), which enables agents to handle microtransactions and recurring payments in stablecoins and fiat
Link Agent Wallet, which will allow agents to pay using Link while controlling spending through approvals
Issuing for Agents — a preview of virtual cards for agents to manage financial workflows
A partnership with Meta which will allow native checkout inside ads on Facebook
We’re here at the conference on the second day to interview John Collison and several other Stripe and Stripe-affiliated executives. See you on the stream.
