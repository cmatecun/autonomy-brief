Executive Summary: SpaceX-Cursor, OpenAI’s new image model, Mythos breach
SpaceX partners with Cursor to “create the world’s best coding and knowledge work AI.” The deal gets Cursor, whose agentic coding model Composer 2 basically operates at frontier-level performance, access to compute [ https://substack.com/redirect/74b743f9-2a75-4b42-8e70-53bd6cf8b24b?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] from SpaceX’s million H100 equivalent Colossus supercomputer. SpaceX gets the right to buy Cursor for $60B later this year (or pay a $10B breakup fee). The announcement comes a week after reporting [ https://substack.com/redirect/f77f379b-70aa-416a-ba03-6e331cdc925c?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] that xAI was planning on supplying Cursor with compute.
OpenAI releases ChatGPT Images 2.0 [ https://substack.com/redirect/4fe65cf0-9405-49d4-97eb-f64f3bedf174?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]. So far the visuals people are making on the timeline are almost shockingly high quality. Arena.ai [ https://substack.com/redirect/ca87a29e-cdcc-4de2-ac6b-e2016e2499ea?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], which measures and ranks frontier AI, now puts GPT Images 2.0 in the #1 spot [ https://substack.com/redirect/bea83a35-62eb-4389-8251-08a0cc212bc1?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] on all their image leaderboards by very large margin: “No model has dominated Image Arena with margins this wide,” they wrote.
A group of unauthorized people have been using Claude Mythos since the day it was released. Big scoop [ https://substack.com/redirect/4075c88f-069f-4d94-9a24-177e65d5d469?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] from Bloomberg’s Rachel Metz: a private Discord used access one of them has a 3rd-party Anthropic contractor to play around with Mythos. The group says they’re just having fun with it. When Anthropic released Mythos two weeks ago, it did so only for a relative few partners, and ran a frankly alarming press cycle that played up how dangerous they say the model is.
