California Trip Data
The California state regulator for ridehail and robotaxis is the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC). As part of their reporting requirements [ https://substack.com/redirect/dc91fbb0-213a-40fa-9f43-0486e4a4872a?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], Waymo is required to submit quarterly reports detailing various operational metrics, including monthly/weekly paid trips, cancelled trips, etc.
The latest release of CPUC data [ https://substack.com/redirect/90f1120a-46d7-4c3a-ab9a-715184b4feca?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] was from February, 2026 (with data through December 2025). Where can I get a Waymo?
Waymo is currently offering paid driverless rides in 11 major cities:
Phoenix, Arizona: Waymo’s first public launch [ https://substack.com/redirect/8d67cabb-5444-46dd-b42d-c66532a95e49?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] happened here in October 2020. Riders can book trips directly through the Waymo app, or through the Uber app by requesting an UberX, Uber Green, Uber Comfort, or Uber Comfort Electric ride and choosing a Waymo vehicle option. Getting matched to a Waymo through the Uber app is subject to availability. African fintech Moove [ https://substack.com/redirect/b9706446-cf40-4840-9e91-7f783047521f?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] runs its fleet operations, facilities and charging infrastructure here — and plans to do the same in Miami soon.
San Francisco Bay Area, California: They opened [ https://substack.com/redirect/40a6cb7b-504b-45b3-bc14-7c5a396a4e66?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] to the public on June 25, 2024. Rides are available exclusively through the Waymo app.
Los Angeles, California: Waymo launched [ https://substack.com/redirect/03ff99c3-1b15-4111-949b-5cff5975c7be?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] in LA on November 12, 2024. Rides are available exclusively through the Waymo app.
Austin, Texas: Public rides began [ https://substack.com/redirect/b7feed56-3f0d-4fd6-9f46-4c7a80f13aae?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] on March 4, 2025, but only through the Uber app. When you request an UberX, Uber Green, Uber Comfort, or Uber Comfort Electric ride, you might get the opportunity to accept a Waymo ride on the confirmation screen, depending on availability.
According to [ https://substack.com/redirect/9d42db2f-dbd4-4cbe-bc80-bde5d9abc358?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] Uber’s CEO, Avomo (formerly Moove Cars) runs fleet management for Uber in Austin - not to be mistaken with Moove.io.
Atlanta, Georgia: Public rides began [ https://substack.com/redirect/f8fe5cc4-2077-4304-9ccc-a76fe157f185?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] here on June 24, 2025. Riders can only hail a Waymo through the Uber App. And like in Austin, there are no guarantees you’ll get a robotaxi every time. Riders need to opt in to the ‘Waymo on Uber’ option and then request an UberX, Uber Comfort, or Comfort Electric — and if a Waymo is available, you’ll get the option to accept the ride. Same as in Austin, Avomo (formerly Moove Cars) runs fleet management for Uber here - not to be mistaken with Moove.io [ https://substack.com/redirect/171a3db2-5b97-4ed1-8b4d-041d96bc2fba?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ].
Miami & Orlando, Florida: Waymo began offering limited driverless rides in Miami on January 22, 2026 (link [ https://substack.com/redirect/dcc6534b-db8b-40d8-a093-0b565ac8df19?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]), and in Orlando on February 24, 2026 (link [ https://substack.com/redirect/0702ec0b-dd7c-4fdc-b039-b7d31118e998?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]). In Miami, they plan to expand service to Miami International Airport later this year, with Moove managing fleet operations, facilities, and charging infrastructure. On April 15, 2026, Waymo opened rides in both cities to the general public, with select Miami riders also gaining access to freeway trips after opting in through the app (link [ https://substack.com/redirect/daf5c3a2-6321-4c75-ba51-cf8e01056aeb?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]).
