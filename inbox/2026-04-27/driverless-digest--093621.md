# Curbivore 2026 Conference - AV Panel Recordings

**From:** Harry from The Driverless Digest <thedriverlessdigest@substack.com>  
**Date:** 2026-04-27T16:36:21.000Z

---

If you missed Curbivore 2026 [ https://substack.com/redirect/4ce4471b-a865-4b66-a5c3-d5cbcbceff11?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] on April 16-17th in downtown Los Angeles, it was our biggest gathering yet, bringing together leaders across autonomy, delivery, and mobility for a full day of panels, firesides, exhibitions, and street food focused on the ideas shaping the future of the curb and cities. You can check out our highlights clip here [ https://substack.com/redirect/1772272d-ffae-4a80-a249-bf7f87d96589?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] to get a feel for the event, and you can find hundreds of our event photos here [ https://substack.com/redirect/c2662904-c414-4ff9-8e82-8ceb0d805d6f?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ].
We also wanted to share recordings from several AV-focused panels and fireside chats as new episodes of The Driverless Digest Podcast [ https://substack.com/redirect/e8546b19-495b-44d3-8675-332f4ef5ec8b?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]. You can find recordings for all the other panels, speeches and fireside chats here [ https://substack.com/redirect/e719c0a8-23d8-4190-a7d2-8a81873214bc?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ].
🎧 Episode 1 — “Urban Autonomy in America: What’s Real & What’s Next [ https://substack.com/redirect/dab752eb-e276-4cc5-ba09-af2e7467b3ab?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]”
Panelists discuss how autonomy has progressed over the last few years, the challenges the industry still faces, and what it will take to accelerate progress as AV services begin to scale. You can listen to/watch this episode on Apple Podcasts [ https://substack.com/redirect/09c113ac-bad7-45f6-9469-af3612699660?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], YouTube [ https://substack.com/redirect/3b1de20f-035a-4b9f-a958-6d94636a8588?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], Spotify [ https://substack.com/redirect/7dfbbac5-b426-446b-99e9-acd550ae0f9a?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], and Substack [ https://substack.com/redirect/dab752eb-e276-4cc5-ba09-af2e7467b3ab?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ].
🎧 Episode 2 — “Keep On Rolling: Electrifying, Scaling & Automating The Vehicles of Tomorrow [ https://substack.com/redirect/4e7ef6c3-bfee-44e0-92f2-f9d81008cde4?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]”
Panelists discuss what it takes to electrify, scale, and automate the vehicles of tomorrow, and the key systems supporting autonomy as deployments expand. You can listen to/watch this episode on Apple Podcasts [ https://substack.com/redirect/42b7f1a5-5994-41be-b730-a097d6995018?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], YouTube [ https://substack.com/redirect/7ac0619b-d405-48ca-978c-cb62d50e63a8?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], Spotify [ https://substack.com/redirect/607749a9-e82f-4f54-a08d-5b9e62b281b2?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], and Substack [ https://substack.com/redirect/4e7ef6c3-bfee-44e0-92f2-f9d81008cde4?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ].
🎧 Episode 3 — “Meet Dot: DoorDash’s Accelerated Autonomous Local Commerce Solution [ https://substack.com/redirect/40e63b08-cd35-4301-855d-cad690bd00a4?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ]”
Harry and DoorDash’s Harrison Shih, dive into DoorDash’s push into autonomy, with a focus on their latest delivery robot, DOT, and how it fits into a broader shift toward multimodal delivery. You can listen to/watch this episode on Apple Podcasts [ https://substack.com/redirect/fead3df4-8d46-48cd-800c-24a1bdcfff60?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], YouTube [ https://substack.com/redirect/ca4426bd-c8a4-4086-ab1e-dba8518666af?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], Spotify [ https://substack.com/redirect/242523d5-1296-42b0-bc83-f248972425db?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ], and Substack [ https://substack.com/redirect/40e63b08-cd35-4301-855d-cad690bd00a4?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ].
What’s next? We’re bringing that same energy to our next Urban Autonomy Summit [ https://substack.com/redirect/09acbb0d-fa94-4b15-9359-8122d90817b1?j=eyJ1IjoiMXlnZjcifQ.KOUIeBfeCRzhIwFnKXyQigR4j0VpF-SZzascYhSZunw ] in Detroit on June 9. After that, we’ll be in Austin this fall and back in San Francisco in the winter for our next series of AV summits.
